starsmashertools.lib.ray¶

class Ray(position: list | tuple | ndarray, direction: list | tuple | ndarray)[source]¶

A ray consists of a 3D position and a 3D direction.

Parameters:

  • position (list, tuple, numpy.ndarray) – The 3D position vector of the ray, (x, y, z).

  • direction (list, tuple, numpy.ndarray) – The 3D direction vector of the ray, \((\hat{x},\hat{y},\hat{z})\). The direction can be obtained as a subtraction of two vectors \(\vec{A}-\vec{B}\), where \(\vec{A}\) is the “destination” and \(\vec{B}\) is the “origin”. This array is normalized automatically.
